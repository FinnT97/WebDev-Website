About

Who we are

SalesAgility is an employee-owned, ISO9001 and 27001 accredited open source CRM Consultancy. All our people are highly invested in developing robust and innovative CRM solutions for clients locally, nationally and globally.

SalesAgility began life delivering bespoke open source solutions for the SugarCRM Community Edition over a decade ago. When SugarCRM abandoned open source in 2013, SalesAgility forked Community Edition and released SuiteCRM as a completely open source CRM solution that delivered all the functionality expected of an enterprise-class CRM application. SuiteCRM is now generally acknowledged to be the world's leading open source CRM solution.

SalesAgility drives revenue from SuiteCRM consulting, development, migration and support projects. We deliver projects and take full responsibility for them. We do this for very large corporates and for smaller enterprises. Our Professional Services work with your teams to ensure project delivery yields business success. We specialise in making complex projects successful ones.

SalesAgility is deeply committed to committed to open source. We have stated on numerous occasions that SuiteCRM is an open source project, that Salesagility is an open source company and that every line of code published by the project will be free and open source. We believe that open source is in the best long term interests of all of our clients and is the future of business applications.
